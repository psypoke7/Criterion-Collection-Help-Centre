Apparel

The Criterion Collection sells a variety of merchandise that you can buy to show your love for our brand.

Our Apparel

We sell The Criterion Collection merchandise through our Gift Shop. On our Gift Shop, you can find:

  • T-shirts
  • Tote and canvas bags
  • Hats
  • Beach towels postcards
  • Mugs

We also work with artists and labels to sell items that feature their branding. Merchandise featuring artists and labels include:

  • Agnès Varda tote bags with illustrations by Christophe Vallaux
  • T-shirts, hats, canvas bags, and mugs by Janus Films

Wrong Item

After ordering merchandise, you might find that it doesn’t fit you or that it doesn’t fit your expectations.

To learn more about returning an item, see Returning an Item.

Restock Frequncy

When browsing your merchandise, you may find that items are unavailable or out of stock.

We can’t estimate when items will return due to uncontrollable factors, such as manufacturing delays. If you want to ask about a specific item, you can send us an email (see Contact Us for more information).

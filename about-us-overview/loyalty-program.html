Our Loyalty Program

Our loyalty program rewards loyal customers for the money they spend at The Criterion Collection.

One dollar equals one point. When you collect 500 points, you receive a $50 gift certificate that you can use on your next purchase.

You earn points when you spend money on:

  • Films
  • Merchandise

You cannot earn points when you spend money on:

  • Shipping fees
  • Sales tax
  • Gift certificates

Joining Our Loyalty Program

You automatically join our loyalty program when you create an account with The Criterion Collection. You can start earning points from your first purchase.

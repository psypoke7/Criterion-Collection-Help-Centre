About The Criterion Collection

Since 1984, over 1000 films have entered The Criterion Collection. We aim to release a wide range of films that highlight the power of cinematic storytelling.

The Criterion Collection Label

The Criterion Collection is continuing series of important classic and contemporary films on home video. Since 1984, our editions include restored film transfers along with special, supplemental features like commentaries, interviews, and essay booklets.

We work closely with filmmakers, scholars, and critics to make sure each film is presented as its director wants it seen and is published in an edition that deepens your appreciation of the film and the art of cinema.

How We Select Our Titles

Our selections aim to reflect all kinds of cinematic expression. We do not want to be snobby about what kinds of films are appropriate for the Criterion label. An arthouse classic, a Hollywood blockbuster, and cult horror film are all eligible, however each title must be an exemplary film of its kind.

How We Announce Upcoming Titles

We announce all our upcoming titles on our website and social media pages as soon as we make them public. We also announce upcoming titles in our newsletter, where you can sign up for special offers and giveaways.

If you would like to suggest a specific title, please write to suggestions@criterion.com. Though we appreciate hearing from you, we are not able to reply to all suggestions.

The Criterion Closet

We host filmmakers and actors in our physical media library to discuss their favourite criterion releases. You can watch Criterion Closet videos on our website, social media accounts, and Youtube page.

There is a mobile version of the Criterion Closet you can visit at select film festivals and venues. You can plan a visit to the mobile Criterion Closet here.

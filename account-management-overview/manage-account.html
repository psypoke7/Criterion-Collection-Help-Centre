Managing an Account

This article explains how to update your account information, view your order history and wish list, and delete your account if needed.

Updating Account Information

You can update account information such as your name, email address, and password.

To update your account information

  1. Select the account icon.
  2. Select Dashboard from the dropdown menu.
  3. Select Edit below your email address.
  4. In the form that appears, update your name, email address, or password.
  5. Select Save Changes.
  6. Your updated information is saved to your account.

Deleting or Deactivating an Account

You can't delete your Criterion Collection account directly through the site. To delete your account, you need to contact our support team and request an account deletion.

To request an account deletion

  1. Go to the Contact Us page.
  2. Select Contact Support.
  3. Request to delete your account and include your registered email address.
  4. Our support team will begin the deletion process and confirm via email.

Caution: Deleting your account permanently removes your personal information, saved items, and order history. This action cannot be undone.

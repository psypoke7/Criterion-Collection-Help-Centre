Shopping

This article explains how to browse titles, manage your wishlist, and complete the checkout process.

Browsing Titles & Collections

Use the search bar or homepage categories to browse titles and collections. You can search up information by title, director, or collection. Product pages include detailed information about each film, edition, and included supplements.

To browse by collection

  1. Select the hamburger menu.
  2. Select the category that interests you.
  3. Use filters and sorting to narrow the search.
  4. Select the film you are interested in.

Checking Out

Once you have decided to purchase a film, there are different options to choose from on the product page. For example, preorder through 4K+ BLU-RAY or BLU-RAY. Choose the option you want and select preorder.

To complete the purchase

  1. Select the cart icon.
  2. Select Check Out.
  3. Follow the prompts to enter shipping and payment information
  4. A confirmation email will be sent after a successful checkout.

Wishlisting

To use the wishlist, you need to create an account. Once you have an account, you can select add to wishlist on any product page to add the title to your wishlist. You can view or edit your wishlist from your account dashboard.

Disc Care

Blu-rays are extremely sensitive to dirt and fingerprints. You must properly store and care for your discs to avoid playback problems.

Handling & Storing Discs

You should keep Blu-ray discs in a cool, dry place. Store them vertically in their original Criterion cases and keep them away from direct sunlight or excessive heat, humidity, and dust.

Cleaning & Caring for Discs

Even small fingerprints and flecks of dirt can cause playback problems.

To clean your Blu-ray

  1. Thoroughly wash your hands.
  2. Hold the Blu-ray disc under cool running water.
  3. Use your thumb or fingertip to gently clean the disc’s surface, rub in a circular motion.
  4. Sandwich the disc between several layers of paper towel.
  5. Gently pat the disc until the paper towel absorbs all the water from the disc’s surface.
  6. The disc is dry and ready to play.
